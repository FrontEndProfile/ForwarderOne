About ForwarderOne

What services do you offer?

We are a freight forwarder for ocean, air and land (rail or truck) international shipping. We specialize in servicing e-commerce businesses to ship cargo from China to warehouses in the US, Canada, EU and UK.


We do both LCL and FCL shipping. We can connect our customers with suppliers that insure cargo, customs brokers, and origin cargo inspections. We have a digital platform where our customers can track shipments, organize their documents, and optimize their processes. Learn more in our Why ForwarderOne? section.

What makes you different from other freight forwarders?

We are a digital freight forwarder in the e-commerce sector. In other words, we focus many of our efforts on developing efficient systems based on technology such as an online portal, international shipment tracking, automated quoting that optimize shipping cost for our customers.

We also specialize in the e-commerce sector. Our founders have built successful D2C brands on Amazon FBA, eBay, Shopify, Walmart, etc. Understanding the e-commerce business operation, import and export matters enables our customers to build a more solid supply chain. Learn more in our Why ForwarderOne? section.

We just started an Amazon FBA business, are we too small to your service?

We service Amazon FBA sellers of any size. No matter you just started your business or you are a mature seller, we will help you ship your product at the best rate.

How can I hire one of your services?

New customers work with an expert to design the best logistic solution for their freight needs, while our active clients can quote from our platform or contact their sales executives. Our team adapts to the communication preferences of both current and new clients.

Reach out to us at service@forwarderone.com.

How much experience does ForwarderOne have in logistics?

Although ForwarderOne started in 2020, some of our experts have more than 10 years working in shipping lines, carriers, and other freight forwarders. Our co-founders knew about the logistics pain points, day-to-day activities and e-commerce business operation before developing ForwarderOne.

Where did ForwarderOne start?

We opened our first office in Yiwu, China. We currently have offices in Yiwu, Shanghai and Las Vegas. We have inbound warehouses close to major logistic hubs in Ningbo, Shanghai, Yiwu and Shenzhen.

About shipments

What kind of commodities can I import or export with ForwarderOne?

Commodities that we do not ship include but are not limited to the following:
Food & drugs
Liquid, powder, or cream
Cigarettes, e-cigarettes
Animal related products
Products that infringe copyrights or patents
Anti-dumping products
Any other products that do not meet local import laws and regulations

Send us an email at service@forwarderone.com if you are not sure.

What are the minimum shipping requirements?

Ocean regular line - 2 cubic meters+
Ocean fast line - 101kg+
Regular Air - 15kg+
Express Air - no limit

Can you insure my cargo?

Yes. We charge 1% of product value as insurance premium and we will cover the product value plus shipping fee should there be any loss to the cargo. However we do not cover damage of the goods as we believe customers or suppliers are responsible for the proper packaging of the cargo.
